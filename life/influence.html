1 Influence

1.1 Do not start boring conversations.

Don't ask 'so what do you do' or 'where are you from'. Aim for non-standard, deeper or more personal stuff.

1.1.1 Deeper questions

1.1.1.1 Tell me about you.
1.1.1.2 What's your story?
1.1.1.3 What personal passion project are you working on right now?
1.1.1.4 Working on anything exciting lately?
1.1.1.5 How do you know the host?

Obviously at a party, conference etc.

1.1.1.6 What's your biggest fear?
1.1.1.7 If you had to pick a book, movie or TV show character that's similar to you, who would it be?
1.1.1.8 What's your biggest regeret?
1.1.1.9 What was your dream job while growing up? Has it come true?
